SPORTS MANAGEMENT · iOS & ANDROID

Youth Sports Audio Experience App

A mobile app that gives youth baseball players AI-generated walk-up intros — designed privacy-first for minors.

PLATFORM

iOS & Android Mobile App

WORK

Product Design · UX Research · UX/UI

FOCUS

Privacy for minors

THE PROBLEM

Giving every kid a "big-league" walk-up moment had to be effortless for volunteers to set up — and safe, because the users are minors.

PROCESS

01

Designed for volunteer setup

Cut setup from 5 steps to 3 (~40% fewer).

02

Privacy-first for minors

No public profiles; data minimized by default.

03

One-tap playback

A game-day flow simple enough to run from the dugout.

04

AI walk-up intros

Generated announcements that sound like the real thing.

OVERVIEW

I designed a mobile experience where a volunteer can set up a team in minutes and trigger AI-generated player intros with one tap — all built around protecting the kids who use it.
